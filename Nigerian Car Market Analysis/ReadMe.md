##  Project Overview

This project analyzes the Nigerian automobile market using vehicle listing data to uncover pricing trends, vehicle characteristics, and consumer preferences. The dashboard provides an interactive overview of the market, helping potential buyers, dealerships, and business stakeholders understand how vehicle prices, mileage, engine specifications, transmission types, and brands vary across Nigeria.

The report transforms raw vehicle listing data into actionable insights that support purchasing decisions, market analysis, and inventory planning.

---

##  Business Objective

The objective of this project was to answer important business questions such as:

- Which car brands dominate the Nigerian market?
- What is the average selling price of vehicles?
- Which vehicle condition is more common?
- What transmission type do Nigerians prefer?
- Which vehicle colors are most popular?
- How do engine size, mileage, and horsepower compare across vehicles?
- How are vehicle listings distributed across Nigerian states?

##  Key Insights

- Toyota and Lexus account for a significant share of vehicle listings.
- Automatic transmission vehicles dominate the market.
- Foreign Used vehicles make up a large proportion of available listings.
- Lagos and the Federal Capital Territory contain the highest concentration of vehicle listings.
- Neutral colors such as Black, White, and Silver are the most commonly listed vehicle colors.
- SUVs and Sedans represent the majority of vehicles available for sale.

---

##  Recommendations

Dealerships should prioritize stocking high-demand brands such as Toyota and Lexus while focusing on automatic transmission vehicles in neutral colors. Pricing strategies should also consider regional demand differences to improve inventory turnover and customer satisfaction.

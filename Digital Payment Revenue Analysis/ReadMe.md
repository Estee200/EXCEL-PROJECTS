## Project Overview

This project analyzes payment transactions processed through Wall Bills Africa Limited to provide management with an interactive financial dashboard for monitoring transaction performance, revenue generation, banking channels, and payment trends.

The dashboard transforms raw payment gateway exports into meaningful business insights that support financial reporting and operational decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Payment transaction records were stored as raw exports containing thousands of individual transactions across multiple banks, payment channels, and customers.

Without an interactive reporting solution, management faced challenges answering important business questions such as:

- Which banking channels generate the most revenue?
- Which payment methods are used most frequently?
- How much revenue is collected every month?
- Which days record the highest transaction activity?
- How much profit is generated from transaction fees?

This project addresses these challenges by consolidating financial data into an executive dashboard.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains payment transactions exported from a real world company's payment gateway in Lagos.

Key fields include:

- Transaction Date
- Customer
- Amount Paid
- Paystack Fees
- Total Fees
- Amount Due
- Settlement Date
- Card Bank
- Card Type
- Payment Channel
- Receiver Bank
- Status
- Transaction ID

## Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Which banks generate the highest revenue?
- How much revenue is collected every month?
- Which days receive the highest number of payments?
- Which payment channel contributes the most revenue?
- What is the total transaction value processed?
- How much income is generated from transaction fees?

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among a few major banking institutions.
- Dedicated NUBAN accounts generate the majority of payment collections.
- Friday records the highest transaction volume during the week.
- Monthly revenue shows noticeable fluctuations, indicating seasonal payment behaviour.
- Transaction fees provide an additional source of income alongside payment collections.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Monitor high-performing banking partners to maintain transaction reliability.
- Investigate low-performing periods to identify operational or customer behaviour changes.
- Continue optimizing dedicated account payment channels due to their high contribution.
- Use monthly revenue trends for forecasting cash inflows and operational planning.
- Track transaction fee income separately to better evaluate profitability.
